Sebastien Le Jeune : Work Journal

Day_1

BA [Hons] Graphic Design

Describe your day

I came in at 10am to the camera equipment room with Adrian and Peter (Staff). To start we were given a safety talk and a tutorial on how to handle the equipment. After the break Peter talked us through the project which was to take photographs of carrots using different verbal prompts - e.g. "slice", "follow", "squeeze". I was taken back to the equipment room to take photos test the lights and experiment with different props. After lunch I sketched up the different ideas I had for the carrot project.

Activities

Safety talk and equipment use tutorial Taking photographs of props using different lightings Prepare for carrot project Sketch ideas for project Library Exercise

What did you learn?

I learned how to make photos look better by using exposure, shadows, light, gradients and angles.
