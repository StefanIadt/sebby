Describe your day

I took the dart to be in the sound studio for 10am. I met the student I would be shadowing for the day Jack Oneill. He took me to a lecture where we learned about different soundtracks in movies and how to create them. We took a quick break then headed into the computer lab. In the lab the teacher assigned the class a project which was to create a piece of music and sound effects for a clip in a game , this went on until lunch. After the lunch break Jack took me into the studio where I watched the class recreate a song from Radiohead. They head to make a parody of the song over 12 weeks using the studio.

Activities

Lecture class Assignment in computer lab Music production in studio

What did you learn?

I learned about the affects of music in a scene, how to use different software and what the course entails
