Sebastien Le Jeune : Work Journal

Day_3

DL838 BA [Hons]Film + Television Production

Describe your day

I showed up to the TV studio set at around 10, and Dave from the staff showed me the cameras and different lighting techniques. All day, they were setting up for a rehearsal for the TV show they were making. Soon after, I headed up to the gallery, and the students showed me around. From the gallery, I watched the first rehearsal and observed how the gallery and director affected what was happening downstairs. After the break, I went back up to the gallery. Later on, I was asked to go back down to the studio. In the studio, I was made into one of the guests on their game show, so I got a taste of acting. It was really fun!

Activities

Camera and light setup gallery work acting test

What did you learn?

I learned how to use cameras and lights effectively . I also learned what the director and gallery does during a shoot.
